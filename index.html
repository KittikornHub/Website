Research Focus

Core themes

The research focus is interdisciplinary, connecting logistics, digital innovation, technology adoption, and healthcare supply chain governance.

Vaccine Supply Chain Visibility

Exploring how timely and reliable information supports vaccine distribution, monitoring, and coordination.

Digital Traceability

Examining traceability mechanisms that may strengthen transparency across healthcare supply chain actors.

Blockchain Adoption

Investigating organizational, technological, and contextual factors associated with blockchain adoption.

Healthcare Logistics

Considering operational challenges in health supply chains, especially in resource-constrained contexts.

Information Systems

Studying how information systems shape decision-making, trust, data quality, and inter-organizational collaboration.

Thailand and LMIC Contexts

Focusing on settings where supply chain visibility may be essential for access, resilience, and public health outcomes.
